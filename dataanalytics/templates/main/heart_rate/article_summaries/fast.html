Summary of fast heart rate (tachycardia) article

This term (tachycardia) is a medical term of fast heart rate. Based on data analysis, your heart rate is conditioned as fast heart rate. In tachycardia condition, you can be healthy or not healthy . Why? There's a lot of factor, but we must exclude some factors, because this analysis is based on heart rate condition.

- not healthy tachycardia

People can be not healthy, because it affect their body. There's some symptoms that can make people not healthy, such as palpitations (sometimes discribed as heart flutter) and feeling dizzy, weakness, anxiety, and sensation of almost passing out. You need ask specialist and take some treatment to take care about this.

- healthy tachycardia

Sometimes, people can healthy in this condition. Mostly, our heart rate is conditioned as fast heart rate not far after excercise. Our heart rate also increased in certain situation, such as consuming caffein, fever, anxiety etc. It's normal, because our heart rate is recovers rapidly to normal heart rate.
