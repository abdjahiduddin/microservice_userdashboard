Summary of slow heart rate (bradycardia) article

This term (bradycardia) is a medical term of slow heart rate. Based on data analysis, your heart rate is conditioned as slow heart rate. In bradycardia condition, you can be healthy or not healthy. Why? There's a lot of factor, but we must exclude some factors, because this analysis is based on heart rate condition.

- not healthy bradycardia

People can be not healthy, because it affect their body. There's some symptoms that can make people not healthy, such as shortness of breath and feeling dizzy and fatigue. You need ask specialist and take some treatment to take care about this.

- healthy bradycardia

Sometimes, people can healthy in this condition. Mostly it's an athlete. Their heart rate can reach 40 - 60 bpm, which is lower than usual people. It's normal for athletes, because they've trained for some activity.
